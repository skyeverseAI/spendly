Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what is needed to provide the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third-party services. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are contractually prohibited from accessing or using your data for any other purpose.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us via the contact information provided on the site. We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries promptly.
